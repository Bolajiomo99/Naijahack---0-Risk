Device Support

Maths Clinic supports all devices running Android 4.3 and above. Unfortunately there are too many devices for us to test them individually. If you have a device that is not working, please let us know and we will attempt to troubleshoot. Below is a list of devices that we have personally tested and some more general information about the support.

List of Supported Devices

These are devices that have been tested internally by Maths Clinic. There are many other compatible devices, and in general a device from the same brand with the same Android version is likely to work, but there may be edge cases where failure occurs.
